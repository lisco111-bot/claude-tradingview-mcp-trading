AMIT DRT ALONE 2 - STRATEGY DOCUMENTATION

Based on Ali Khan's ICT (Inner Circle Trader) and DRT (Dealing Range Theory) Methodology

STRATEGY OVERVIEW:
This strategy combines Ali Khan's Dealing Range Theory with ICT concepts for high-probability trading.

CORE COMPONENTS:

1. DEALING RANGE THEORY (DRT)
   - Type 1 Range: Consolidation before expansion
   - Type 2 Range: Retracement after initial move
   - Type 3 Range: Final range before reversal
   - Quadrant Levels: 25DRT, 50DRT, 75DRT, 100DRT

2. POWER OF 3 (PO3)
   - Accumulation: Quiet accumulation phase
   - Manipulation: Smart money manipulation phase
   - Distribution: Final distribution phase

3. FAIR VALUE GAP (FVG)
   - Three-candle pattern with unfilled gap
   - Price often returns to fill FVG
   - Acts as support/resistance level

4. LIQUIDITY CONCEPTS
   - Buy-side liquidity: Stop loss orders below key levels
   - Sell-side liquidity: Stop loss orders above key levels
   - Sweep: Price runs through liquidity to trigger orders

ENTRY CONDITIONS:
1. Market structure confirms HH/LL pattern
2. Type 3 dealing range identified
3. External liquidity has been swept
4. FVG present inside dealing range
5. PO3 in distribution phase

ENTRY TIMING:
- Enter on pullback after liquidity sweep
- Wait for price to close back inside dealing range
- Confirm alignment of bias, structure, and liquidity

EXIT RULES:
- Stop Loss: 50 pips (fixed for all symbols)
- Take Profit: 150 pips (fixed for all symbols)
- Partial Profit: 50% at 75 pips, remaining 50% at 150 pips

RISK MANAGEMENT:
- Max trades per day: 20
- Max trade size: $2,500
- Portfolio value: $50
- Position size: 10% of portfolio
- Risk per trade: 2%

TRADE EXECUTION:
1. Identify market structure and trend
2. Locate appropriate dealing range type
3. Wait for liquidity sweep
4. Confirm FVG presence
5. Check PO3 phase alignment
6. Enter on pullback
7. Set stop loss at 50 pips
8. Set take profit at 150 pips

RECORD KEEPING:
All trades automatically recorded in trades.csv with:
- Date and time
- Symbol and side
- Entry/exit prices
- Profit/loss amounts
- Order ID
- Notes on strategy conditions

STRATEGY VALIDATION:
- Only execute when ALL conditions align
- No partial conditions accepted
- Market structure is primary filter
- Liquidity sweep is key timing signal
